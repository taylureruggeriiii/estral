    Your cycle is data.

    Everything you need to know about tracking and why it changes everything.

    Tip from ESTRAL

    A healthy menstrual cycle
    Length
    21–35 days is normal. 28 days is the average — not the rule. Your cycle is yours.
    Bleed
    3–7 days. Flow goes from moderate to light. Clots smaller than a 10p coin are normal. Pain that stops your life is not.
    Hormones
    Oestrogen rises through follicular, peaks at ovulation. Progesterone takes over in luteal. If either flatlines, you feel it — mood, energy, sleep, all of it.
    Signs of imbalance
    Severe PMS, cycles under 21 or over 35 days, missing periods, extreme fatigue, or pain that stops you — these are signals, not your normal.
    How to track
    Tip from ESTRAL

    start with just one thing: the first day of your bleed. that is day 1. log it every month. after 2–3 cycles you will see your pattern. everything else — mood, energy, symptoms, sleep — builds from that single data point. the app does the rest.

    1️⃣
    Log day 1 of your period
    The first day of real flow — not spotting. This resets your cycle clock.
    2️⃣
    Note your energy daily
    Just a number. 1–10. After a month you will see it move with your phases.
    3️⃣
    Track symptoms without judgment
    Bloating, mood dips, brain fog, cravings. These are messages, not problems.
    4️⃣
    Be consistent for 3 cycles
    That is when the patterns become undeniable. That is when ESTRAL gets sharp.
    Using the moon until your data builds
    Tip from ESTRAL

    before women had cycle tracking apps, they had the moon. a healthy cycle is approximately 29.5 days — the same as a lunar month. this is not a coincidence. the moon is an external rhythm you can sync to until your internal one becomes clear.

    🌑
    New Moon → Menstrual
    Rest. Release. Turn inward. The slate clears. Low energy is purposeful — your body is doing deep work. Do not fight it.
    🌒
    Waxing Crescent → Follicular
    Energy builds. New ideas emerge. Start things. Your oestrogen mirrors the growing light — curiosity, creativity, and momentum return.
    🌕
    Full Moon → Ovulation
    Peak energy. Peak magnetism. Be seen. Pitch, connect, launch, show up fully. This is your most outward, electric window.
    🌘
    Waning Moon → Luteal
    Edit, refine, close loops. Your inner critic gets loud — use that precision. Finish what follicular started. Say no to what does not belong.

    When your next period starts, log it in the app. That is step one. Everything else builds from there.

    Your rhythm
    Tip from ESTRAL

    here is everything you need to know about tracking, what a healthy cycle looks like, and why understanding your rhythm changes everything.

    Understanding
    your cycle.

    A healthy menstrual cycle
    Length
    21–35 days is normal. 28 days is average but not the rule. Your cycle is healthy if it is consistent for you.
    Bleed
    3–7 days. Light to moderate flow. Some cramping is normal — debilitating pain is not. Your bleed is data, not an inconvenience.
    Energy pattern
    Energy naturally rises through follicular into ovulation, then softens through luteal into bleed. If yours feels flat all cycle — that is worth paying attention to.
    Why tracking changes everything
    Tip from ESTRAL

    your cycle is the most honest data you have about your body. when you track it — even just the first day of your bleed — you start to see patterns. why you feel unstoppable some weeks and completely offline others. why certain conversations wreck you at certain times. why your hunger, your sleep, your creativity all move in waves. this is not woo. this is biology. and once you see it, you cannot unsee it.

    How to track — start here
    1.
    Day 1 is the first day of your bleed — not spotting, actual flow. Log it here in ESTRAL every cycle. That single data point builds your whole picture.
    2.
    Note how you feel each day — energy, mood, sleep, body. You do not need to be detailed. Even one word is enough. Over time patterns emerge on their own.
    3.
    Do not try to fix or optimise yet — just observe. Three cycles of honest tracking will show you more about yourself than any productivity book.
    Using the moon until your data builds
    Tip from ESTRAL

    before your cycle data is established, the moon is your rhythm map. every woman historically lived by it — before artificial light disrupted our internal clocks, most women bled at the new moon and ovulated at the full moon. you may not sync perfectly, but the energy of each phase mirrors your own.

    🌑
    New Moon
    Menstrual energy. Rest, release, turn inward. Not the time to launch or decide.
    🌒
    Waxing Crescent
    Follicular rising. Set intentions, start new things, energy building.
    🌕
    Full Moon
    Ovulatory peak. Maximum energy and magnetism. Be seen, connect, launch.
    🌘
    Waning
    Luteal wind-down. Finish, edit, release what is not working. Go quieter.
